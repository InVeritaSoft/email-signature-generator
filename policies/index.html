Privacy Policy for Email Signature Generator Chrome Extension

Last Updated: November 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Email Signature Generator Chrome Extension ("the Extension", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Data Collection and Storage

What Data We Collect

The Extension collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Personal Information You Provide:
    • Name
    • Job title
    • Contact information (LinkedIn profile URL, website URL, social media links)
    • Custom text for links
    • Uploaded profile images (converted to base64 format)
    • Selected signature style/template preference

How Data is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's Storage API (chrome.storage.local). This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • Your data is not transmitted to any external servers
  • Your data is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Your data is stored in your browser's local storage

Data Synchronization

If you have Chrome Sync enabled, your signature data may be synchronized across your devices through Google's Chrome Sync service. This synchronization is handled entirely by Google Chrome and is subject to Google's privacy policy. We do not have access to this synchronized data.

Data Usage

How We Use Your Data

The Extension uses your stored data only for the following purposes:

  • To generate your email signature based on the information you provide
  • To remember your preferences and signature settings between sessions
  • To allow you to copy your generated signature to your clipboard

What We Do NOT Do

We do NOT:

  • Send your data to external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Collect analytics or usage statistics
  • Access any information beyond what you explicitly enter in the extension

Permissions

The Extension requests the following permissions:

1. Storage Permission

  • Purpose: To save your signature data locally so you don't have to re-enter it each time
  • Data Stored: Only the signature information you explicitly enter
  • Location: Stored locally on your device using Chrome Storage API

2. Clipboard Write Permission

  • Purpose: To copy your generated email signature to your clipboard
  • Usage: Only when you click the "Copy" button in the extension
  • Data: Only the generated HTML signature is copied; no other clipboard data is accessed

Third-Party Services

The Extension uses the following third-party services:

Google Fonts

  • Service: Google Fonts (fonts.gstatic.com)
  • Purpose: To load web fonts for displaying the extension interface
  • Data: No personal data is sent to Google Fonts. Only font file requests are made.
  • Privacy: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • No external servers are involved in data storage or processing
  • The Extension works entirely offline after initial installation

Your Rights and Control

You have full control over your data:

Access Your Data

  • All your signature data is visible in the extension's interface
  • You can view and edit all stored information at any time

Delete Your Data

  • You can delete your signature data at any time by clicking the "Reset" button in the extension
  • You can uninstall the extension, which will remove all stored data from your device
  • Data stored via Chrome Sync will be removed according to Chrome's sync policies

Export Your Data

  • You can copy your generated signature HTML at any time
  • Your signature data is stored in Chrome's local storage, which you can access through Chrome's developer tools if needed

Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

In simple terms:

  • All your data stays on your device
  • We don't collect, share, or sell your information
