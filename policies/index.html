Privacy Policy for Email Signature Generator Chrome Extension

Last Updated: November 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how InVeritaSoft ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Email Signature Generator Chrome Extension ("Extension"). This Policy applies to all users of the Extension and governs our data practices in compliance with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

By installing and using the Extension, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not use the Extension.

Information Collection and Processing

Types of Information Collected

The Extension collects and processes the following categories of information, all of which is stored exclusively on your local device:

  • Personal Information You Provide:
    • Name
    • Job title
    • Contact information (LinkedIn profile URL, website URL, social media links)
    • Custom text for links
    • Uploaded profile images (converted to base64 format)
    • Selected signature style/template preference

Data Storage and Retention

All information collected by the Extension is stored exclusively on your local device using Google Chrome's Storage API (chrome.storage.local). We implement the following storage practices:

  • Local Storage Only: All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers or third-party services
  • No Remote Access: We do not have, and cannot obtain, access to any data stored on your device
  • Browser-Based Storage: Data is stored within your browser's local storage system, subject to your browser's storage policies
  • User-Controlled Retention: You may delete all stored data at any time through the Extension's reset functionality or by uninstalling the Extension

Data Synchronization

If you have enabled Google Chrome Sync on your account, your Extension data may be synchronized across your devices through Google's Chrome Sync service. This synchronization functionality is:

  • Managed by Google: Synchronization is handled entirely by Google Chrome and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Outside Our Control: We do not control, access, or have visibility into the synchronization process
  • User-Configurable: You may disable Chrome Sync at any time through your Chrome browser settings
  • Subject to Google's Terms: Any synchronized data is governed by Google's terms of service and privacy policies

Use of Information

Purpose and Legal Basis for Processing

We process your information solely for the following legitimate purposes, all of which are necessary for the Extension's core functionality:

  • Service Provision: To generate email signatures based on the information you provide
  • User Experience: To maintain your preferences and signature settings between browser sessions
  • Functionality: To enable the copying of generated signatures to your clipboard for use in email clients

The legal basis for processing is your explicit consent, which you provide by using the Extension and entering your information.

Restrictions on Data Use

We expressly commit to the following restrictions:

  • No External Transmission: We do not transmit, upload, or send any data to external servers or third-party services
  • No Data Sharing: We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose your information to any third parties
  • No Commercial Use: We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or any commercial purposes
  • No Tracking: We do not track your browsing activity, website visits, or any online behavior
  • No Analytics: We do not collect analytics, usage statistics, or telemetry data
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only process information that you explicitly provide through the Extension interface

Extension Permissions and Justification

The Extension requires the following permissions to function. Each permission is necessary for core functionality and is used only for the stated purpose:

1. Storage Permission

  • Purpose: To save your signature data locally so you don't have to re-enter it each time
  • Data Stored: Only the signature information you explicitly enter
  • Location: Stored locally on your device using Chrome Storage API

2. Clipboard Write Permission

  • Purpose: To copy your generated email signature to your clipboard
  • Usage: Only when you click the "Copy" button in the extension
  • Data: Only the generated HTML signature is copied; no other clipboard data is accessed

Third-Party Services

The Extension uses the following third-party services:

Google Fonts

  • Service: Google Fonts (fonts.gstatic.com)
  • Purpose: To load web fonts for displaying the extension interface
  • Data: No personal data is sent to Google Fonts. Only font file requests are made.
  • Privacy: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy

Data Security and Protection

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Storage Architecture: All data is stored exclusively on your device using secure browser storage mechanisms
  • No Network Transmission: No data is transmitted over the internet or stored on remote servers
  • No External Processing: All data processing occurs locally on your device
  • Offline Functionality: The Extension operates entirely offline after initial installation, eliminating network-based security risks
  • Browser Security: Data is protected by your browser's built-in security features and Chrome's storage API security model

While we implement these security measures, please note that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and browser.

Your Privacy Rights

In accordance with applicable privacy laws, including GDPR and CCPA, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right of Access

  • You have the right to access all personal information stored by the Extension
  • All stored data is accessible and editable through the Extension's user interface
  • You may view, review, and modify your information at any time

Right to Deletion

  • You have the right to request deletion of your personal information
  • You may delete all Extension data immediately by using the "Reset" functionality within the Extension
  • Uninstalling the Extension will remove all locally stored data from your device
  • Data synchronized via Chrome Sync will be removed in accordance with Google Chrome's synchronization policies

Right to Data Portability

  • You have the right to export your data in a portable format
  • You may copy your generated signature HTML at any time for use in other applications
  • Your signature data stored in Chrome's local storage may be accessed through browser developer tools if needed

Right to Withdraw Consent

  • You may withdraw your consent to data processing at any time by uninstalling the Extension
  • Withdrawal of consent will result in the deletion of all locally stored data
